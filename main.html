Summary

"Inception" is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan, which explores the concept of entering and manipulating people's dreams. Dom Cobb (Leonardo DiCaprio) is a skilled thief who steals secrets from within the subconscious during the dream state. Given a chance to have his criminal history erased, he must perform the impossible task of "inception" – planting an idea into someone's mind.
